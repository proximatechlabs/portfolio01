About Me

Howdy, y'all! My name is Mubashir Hussain, and I am from Lahore, Pakistan. I got my start as a graphic designer by creating a brouchere for my dad's friend. As locals began to see my work, I began designing materials for everything from western wear to crawdads on a stick. Today, I am open to all kinds of design work, although I am always drawn to passion projects.
